Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and protect your data. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe. It applies to theupkeepgroup.com and related services provided by The Upkeep Group Pty Ltd.

Who we are

The Upkeep Group Pty Ltd (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides services across IT, Facilities, Cleaning, Labour Hire and Finance. You can contact us at hello@theupkeepgroup.com.

Scope

This policy covers information we collect when you use our website, contact us, or engage our services.

Information we collect

Information you provide
  • Contact details (name, email, phone, company)
  • Enquiry details (message content, service interests)
  • Contractual & billing information (when you become a client)
Automatically collected
  • Device & browser metadata
  • IP address and general location
  • Usage analytics (pages visited, actions, timestamps)
From third parties
  • Verification providers (e.g., police check status)
  • Payment & accounting platforms (for invoices & receipts)
  • Recruitment or reference sources (with consent)

How we use information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide proposals
  • To deliver and improve our services
  • To manage billing, payments and accounting
  • To ensure safety, compliance and right-to-work checks
  • To monitor website performance and prevent abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations
Legal bases (where applicable)
  • Consent (e.g., contact forms)
  • Contract performance (service delivery)
  • Legitimate interests (security, service improvement)
  • Legal obligations (tax, employment, safety)

Cookies & analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site and understand traffic. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies; some features may not function properly.

Do-not-track

We honour standard browser privacy controls where feasible.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

Service providers

Trusted vendors who help us deliver services (e.g., hosting, email, payments).

Compliance & safety

Background checks, right-to-work verification, and safety compliance where required.

Legal requirements

Where we must comply with law, regulation, or valid legal requests.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including MFA on systems, access controls, backups and device management. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

Data retention

We keep information as long as needed for the purposes above and to meet legal and accounting requirements. When no longer required, we take steps to delete or de-identify it.
