Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Overview

We respect your privacy. This policy explains how The Upkeep Group Pty Ltd collects, uses, and safeguards personal information in connection with our services and this website.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact details (name, email, phone, company)
  • Service preferences and enquiry details sent via our forms
  • Operational records we need to deliver services (e.g., rosters, checklists)
  • Website analytics (non-identifying, aggregated)

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide our services
  • To improve operations, security and user experience
  • To meet legal, regulatory and safety obligations

4. Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share information with trusted providers (e.g., IT hosting, email, payroll/bookkeeping partners) strictly to deliver services, under confidentiality obligations.

5. Data storage & security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical and physical safeguards (e.g., MFA, access controls, backups). Internet transmission and storage carry inherent risks; no method is 100% secure.

6. Your rights

You may request access, correction or deletion of your personal information (subject to legal exceptions).
